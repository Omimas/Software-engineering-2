1.) Activity Diagram Flow for the Online Shopping System

1. Activity Diagram Flow

Start: The customer enters the system.

Create Account: The customer creates an account (if not already done).

Browse Products: The customer browses products.

Add to Cart: The customer adds products to the shopping cart.

Payment Process: After adding products, the customer proceeds with the payment.

Track Order: After purchase, the customer can track the order.

End: This marks the end of the process.

2. Additional Activities for Seller, Admin, and Support Agent

  • Seller: Adds/removes products, approves sales, dispatches products.
  • Admin: Manages stock, handles orders.
  • Support Agent: Provides after-sales support, handles complaints.

3. Explanation of the Diagram

  • Customer: Represents the customer flow through the system, starting from account creation (or login) to product browsing, adding products to the cart, and proceeding to payment.
  • Seller: After the customer makes the purchase, the seller's tasks such as approving the sale and dispatching the product are captured.
  • Admin: Admin tasks include managing stock and orders.
  • Support Agent: After-sales support and handling complaints by the support agent.
